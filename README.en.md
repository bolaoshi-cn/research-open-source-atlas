# bo's Open-Source Research Capability Atlas

[中文版](README.md)

![bo Research Capability Atlas](assets/bolaoshi-research-atlas.png)

A curated public catalog of 52 open-source research tools, skills, and libraries -- each mapped to the research capabilities it provides, the mechanisms that power it, its known limitations, and the evidence behind each claim. Built to help researchers judge, compare, and adopt tools by what they can actually do, not by star count or marketing.

## Why this is public

Most research-tool lists stop at names and star counts. This atlas catalogs capabilities, mechanisms, limitations, and evidence so that researchers can make informed adoption decisions. Keeping the atlas public and versioned on GitHub makes the curation transparent, reusable, and open to community verification.
